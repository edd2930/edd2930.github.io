Who I am

Located in the Center of Health, Science & Technology

I obtained my B.S. in Microbiology from the University of Michigan. I began doing microbiome research during my gap year before attending the University Michigan School of Public Health. This was my first introduction to the world of big data and using technology as a tool to advance health & science. This inspired me to pursue a certificate in Health Informatics to supplement my Hospital & Molecular Epidemiology M.P.H. degree. After working in the research world full-time, I decided I wanted a role that incorporates more technology skills and coding. From there I took the plunge to teach myself Python3, HTML5, and CSS3.
